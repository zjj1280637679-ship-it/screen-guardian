# Scenario: Short Name

## User Situation

Who needs help and what they are trying to accomplish.

## External Conditions

System state, dependencies, app behavior, privacy expectations, timing, and other constraints.

## Desired Effect

What success means from the user's point of view and from the AI runtime's point of view.

## Recommended Route

The preferred AI-first tool, lower-level fallback, or workflow envelope.

## Guard And Budget

Which guard checks, context policy, runtime limits, storage rules, or optional feature flags apply.

## Failure Branches

How the card handles unavailable dependencies, ambiguous targets, bad captures, privacy-sensitive targets, budget exhaustion, or user-decision states.

## Acceptance Checks

Concrete conditions that would make this scenario pass in validation, smoke testing, or manual review.

## Related Claims

Whitepaper chapters, design concepts, receipt fields, or traceability ids.
